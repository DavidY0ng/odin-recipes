Baked Buffalo Chicken Wings

Crispy buffalo chicken wings don’t have to be deep fried to be delicious! This easy, Crispy Baked Buffalo Chicken Wings recipe makes the perfect appetizer or meal!

Why We Love these Crispy Baked Wings

No Frying Makes them Better for Us – Since these don’t have to spend time cooking in a load of oil, they are a bit better for us than eating the deep-fried version.

Easy to make – With quick prep of fewer than 10 minutes, these are so easy to bake in the oven to enjoy anytime!

Delicious appetizer or simple meal – Great for football parties or a casual meal, these crispy wings are hard to beat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Cornstarch
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Stone House Seasoning
  • Blue Cheese Dressing - optional

Instructions

  1. I preheat my oven and then line a baking sheet with parchment or foil and then arrange a wire rack on top of the lined baking sheet.
  2. For the next step, I whisk together my cornstarch and Stone House Seasoning and then pour it over my chicken wings. Then, I lightly toss the chicken wings in the cornstarch mixture to make sure they are well coated. Since we aren’t frying the chicken wings, the cornstarch serves to help make the chicken wings have a nice crispy, crunchy crust on the outside when they bake.
  3. Then, I bake my chicken wings until the skin is crispy. This usually takes about 50 minutes. I then remove them from the wire rack and toss them in my buffalo sauce. For those who like their buffalo chicken wings wet with sauce, they are ready to be served. For those like me that like a drier buffalo chicken wing, I return them to the oven for another 5 to 8 minutes before serving.
  4. To serve them, place them onto a platter and serve them with blue cheese dressing or ranch dressing for dipping! They are perfect for parties, get-togethers, watching games, and even enjoying as a meal if you like!
